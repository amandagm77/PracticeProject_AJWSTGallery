Purpose of this Gallery

What is the significance of JWST?

The James Webb Space Telescope was launched on Christmas Day, 2021 [1]. Since its launch, it has greatly exceeded the capabilities of its predecessor, the Hubble Space Telescope, due to it being nearly 100 times more sensitive to light capture. This allows us to see much dimmer and farther objects in our universe.[2]

It is important to recognize this progress and the potential it has to strengthen our understanding of our universe, and the study in the field of astronomy as a whole. By sharing these stunning photos captured by JWST, the excitement creates incentive and inspiration to continue the journey of space exploration, both within space itself and from here on Earth with our advancing technology.

Benefits of sharing JWST Images:

  • Easily demonstrates the capabilities of the JWST
  • Creates excitement for astronomy for the general public
  • Accessible to a broader audience
